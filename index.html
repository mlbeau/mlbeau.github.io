About Me

I design and build correctness-first financial infrastructure for regulated domains, where statutory requirements, operational execution, and real-world risk intersect. I am the Founder and Systems Architect at LeanLogic Systems, LLC, and the builder of LeanBuild™ — a compliance correctness layer for construction payments. My work focuses on isolating statutory correctness from workflow execution, enabling payment decisions to be validated, explained, and documented before funds are released, without displacing human judgment or existing systems. LeanBuild exists because deep compliance validation carries liability that workflow platforms cannot economically absorb. I design systems that surface uncertainty explicitly, preserve invariants under real-world conditions, and produce audit-ready evidence rather than opaque automation. Current work is centered on production systems, live market validation, and partner integrations — not public demos.

I am most interested in conversations with workflow platform builders, construction lenders, and fintech infrastructure partners working in liability-constrained environments.

Current Focus

My current work is focused on building LeanBuild, a correctness infrastructure layer for construction payments that operates at the point of payment review — before funds are released. LeanBuild isolates statutory and financial correctness from workflow execution, enabling lien waiver and payment documentation to be validated, explained, and audited without displacing existing accounting systems, workflow platforms, or human judgment.

Correctness Infrastructure & API Design

Designing modular, API-first systems that surface statutory and operational constraints at execution time. Focused on validation layers, confidence-bounded outputs, and integration patterns that complement — rather than replace — existing platforms.

Statutory Validation & Rule Engines

Implementing deterministic, state-specific lien waiver validation logic with explicit uncertainty handling, statutory citation mapping, and correction guidance suitable for regulated, liability-sensitive environments.

Workflow-Adjacent Integration

Integrating with accounting systems and construction workflow platforms to receive documents and events for validation, without embedding business logic or decision authority inside partner systems.

Auditability & Evidence Preservation

Designing data models and validation pipelines that preserve evidence, context, and decision rationale, supporting defensible payment records and downstream review.

Execution-Stage Risk Visibility

Developing analytics that surface degradation patterns, exceptions, and coordination risks at payment review — enabling monitoring and early signal detection rather than enforcement or control.

Founder-to-Founder Advisory

Advising founders and technical leaders building regulated or liability-constrained systems on correctness-first architecture, integration boundaries, and early design decisions that reduce long-term risk.
